Manicure and Nail Treatment Skills

Manicure Treatment

A Manicure Treatment includes cleaning and shaping the nails and pushing back and trimming the cuticles and applying nail polish or acrlyic powder.Some skills you need for a good manicure are ayyention to detail and steady hands for nail artand knowledge of different nail products.

Nail Treatment

Nail Care involves washing the hands, cleaning under the nails and filing any rough edges before applying Acrylics or polish. Be gentle until the acrylic is fully hardened. Advise your client to be gentle with their acrylic nails for the first 24-48 hours. When you remove your acrylics keep your nails hydrated and nourished with cuticle oil , avoiding harsh chemicals and excessive filing , and giving your nails a break from acrylics because your nails become soft and dull after having acrylics.
